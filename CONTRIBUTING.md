# Contributing to Open Decision Receipt

Contributions should make AI-enabled decisions more replayable, evidenced, bounded, and accountable.

## Good contributions

- New fields mapped to a named weakness class, such as CWE, OWASP LLM, STRIDE, or a recognized control failure.
- Worked examples for real workflow types: claims, credit, hiring, code merge, access approval, model deployment, procurement, incident response.
- Regulatory mappings with primary-source references.
- Validation or implementation examples that preserve the schema invariant.

## Acceptance bar

Every proposed field or example should answer at least one of these:

1. What did the human check?
2. What did the system use?
3. What changed between check and use?
4. Who had authority?
5. What evidence was visible?
6. What action actually executed?
7. Who owns the consequence?
8. Can the decision be replayed?

Unverifiable incident claims, vendor marketing, and generic governance language do not belong here.
